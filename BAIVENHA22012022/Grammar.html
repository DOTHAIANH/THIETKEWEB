nouns


A noun represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.


kinds of noun


    Nouns are classified as follows:

  • Proper: It is the name of a particular object, whether person, place, animal,idea
    or thing.
  • Common: It is the name possessed by things in general.
  • Collective: It is the name representing a group of things.
  • Abstract: It is the name representing qualities, emotions, conditions or actions.

pronouns


A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid
repetition of a noun.


kinds of pronoun


    Pronouns are classified as follows:

  • Personal pronouns: It refers to a specific noun and used as a substitue of the
    noun.
  • Interrogative pronouns: It stands as the answer to the question about a
    person, place or a thing.
  • Indefinite pronouns: It is used to refer to a person, thing or a place that are
    unknown.
  • Relative pronouns: It relates to a noun, pronoun, word or expression
    preceding it in the sentence, and at the same time connects two statements.
  • Demonstrative pronouns: A noun or pronoun is specified and identified by it.
  • Reflexive pronouns: It usually follows a noun or a pronoun and used to
    emphasize the subject.
